About NEHOCO

📌 Our Mission

To foster economic self-reliance, advocate for youth rights and well-being, and expand access to education, healthcare, and social support systems.

🎯 Our Vision

A community where disadvantaged youth are empowered, self-reliant, and actively contributing to sustainable development.

Become a Member

✅ Eligibility

  • Kenyan citizen, 18+ years
  • Sound mind & good character
  • No criminal conviction (dishonesty)
  • Share vision of empowering youth

📝 How to Join

  1. Submit written application to Executive Committee
  2. Vetting & 2/3 members approval
  3. Pay non-refundable Ksh 200 registration fee
  4. Receive membership certificate

💰 Member Contributions

Monthly contributions as determined by the group. Failure to remit for 3 consecutive months may lead to disciplinary action.
