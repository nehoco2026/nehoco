Who We Are

🎯

Our Mission

To foster economic self-reliance among disadvantaged youth through skills training, mentorship, and entrepreneurship opportunities.

👁️

Our Vision

A Kenya where every young person has the opportunity to succeed, regardless of their background.

💚

Our Values

Integrity, accountability, inclusivity, discipline, and shared responsibility.

What We Do

🛠️

Skills Training

Tailoring, carpentry, IT, agribusiness, and vocational skills.

🧠

Mentorship

One-on-one guidance connecting youth with successful professionals.

💼

Entrepreneurship

Business training, startup support, and ongoing guidance.

📢

Advocacy

Fighting for youth rights, education, healthcare, and social support.

🤝

Partnerships

Collaborating with government, NGOs, and private sector.

❤️

Social Support

Healthcare referrals, mental health support, and community building.
