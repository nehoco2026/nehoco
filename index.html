Who We Are

🎯

Our Mission

To foster economic self-reliance among disadvantaged youth through skills training, mentorship, and entrepreneurship opportunities in Karatina and beyond.

👁️

Our Vision

A Kenya where every young person has the opportunity to succeed, regardless of their background or circumstances.

💚

Our Values

Integrity, accountability, inclusivity, discipline, and shared responsibility guide everything we do.

Our Activities

🛠️

Skills Training

Tailoring, carpentry, IT, agribusiness, and vocational skills for employability.

🧠

Mentorship

One-on-one and group mentorship connecting youth with successful professionals.

📢

Advocacy

Fighting for youth rights, education, healthcare, and social support.

🤝

Entrepreneurship

Business training, startup support, and ongoing guidance for young entrepreneurs.

🌍

Partnerships

Collaborating with government, NGOs, and private sector to maximize impact.

❤️

Social Support

Healthcare referrals, mental health support, and community building.
